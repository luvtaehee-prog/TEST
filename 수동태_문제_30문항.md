## [정답 및 해설]

### 서술형 (1~20번)

**1.**
- 정답: English is taught to us by a Canadian at school.
- 핵심 문법: 4형식 문장의 직접목적어를 주어로 한 수동태. teach는 직접목적어가 주어일 때 간접목적어 앞에 to를 쓴다.
- 출제 근거: A Canadian teaches us English at school. → English is taught to us by a Canadian at school.
- 감점 포인트: to 누락(English is taught us…), 능동형 taught → 시제·태 오류.

**2.**
- 정답: The pretty mug was bought for me by my friend.
- 핵심 문법: buy는 직접목적어가 주어일 때 간접목적어 앞에 for를 쓴다. 간접목적어(me)를 주어로 한 수동태는 불가.
- 출제 근거: My friend bought me the pretty mug. → The pretty mug was bought for me by my friend.
- 감점 포인트: for 대신 to 사용, I was bought…(간접목적어 주어 불가) 형태.

**3.**
- 정답: Julian was elected to be our leader.
- 핵심 문법: 5형식 목적격 보어(to be…)가 그대로 유지되는 수동태.
- 출제 근거: We elected Julian to be our leader. → Julian was elected to be our leader.
- 감점 포인트: be동사 누락(Julian elected…), to 누락.

**4.**
- 정답: The actor was made popular by his good looks.
- 핵심 문법: 5형식 형용사 목적격 보어가 수동태에서 그대로 유지.
- 출제 근거: His good looks made the actor popular. → The actor was made popular by his good looks.
- 감점 포인트: popular를 부사/명사로 변형, by 누락.

**5.**
- 정답: Maria was seen reading a travel guide by me. (허용: to read)
- 핵심 문법: 지각동사(see)의 목적격 보어로 쓰인 동사원형은 수동태에서 현재분사 또는 to부정사로 바뀐다.
- 출제 근거: I saw Maria read a travel guide. → Maria was seen reading[to read] a travel guide by me.
- 감점 포인트: 원형 read 유지(was seen read), by me 누락.

**6.**
- 정답: I was made to learn taekwondo by my mom.
- 핵심 문법: 사역동사 make의 수동태에서 목적격 보어로 쓰인 동사원형은 to부정사로 바뀐다.
- 출제 근거: My mom made me learn taekwondo. → I was made to learn taekwondo by my mom.
- 감점 포인트: to 누락(was made learn), 원형 유지.

**7.**
- 정답: Esther was made to learn ballet at the age of five.
- 핵심 문법: 사역동사 make 수동태 + to부정사 보어.
- 출제 근거: (부교재 예문) Esther는 다섯 살에 발레를 배우게 되었다. → Esther was made to learn ballet at the age of five.
- 감점 포인트: to 누락.

**8.**
- 정답: The project was carried out in secret.
- 핵심 문법: 동사구(carry out) 수동태는 동사구 전체를 하나의 동사처럼 취급한다.
- 출제 근거: We carried out the project in secret. → The project was carried out in secret (by us).
- 감점 포인트: out 누락(was carried in secret), carried → carry 형태 오류.

**9.**
- 정답: The job offer was turned down by Ray.
- 핵심 문법: 동사구(turn down) 수동태.
- 출제 근거: (부교재 예문) The job offer was turned down by Ray.
- 감점 포인트: down 누락, by 누락.

**10.**
- 정답: It is said that the movie is worth watching.
- 핵심 문법: 목적어가 that절인 문장의 수동태 — 「It + be동사 + p.p. + that ~」.
- 출제 근거: People say that the movie is worth watching. → It is said that the movie is worth watching.
- 감점 포인트: worth 뒤 watched(worth + v-ing), that 누락.

**11.**
- 정답: The movie is said to be worth watching.
- 핵심 문법: that절 주어를 수동태 주어로 올릴 때 that절 동사는 to부정사로 바뀐다.
- 출제 근거: People say that the movie is worth watching. → The movie is said to be worth watching.
- 감점 포인트: to be 누락(is said worth), be 누락.

**12.**
- 정답: The actor is believed to earn more than $10 million every year.
- 핵심 문법: that절 주어의 수동태 전환 + to부정사.
- 출제 근거: (부교재 예문) 그 배우는 매년 천만 달러 이상을 번다고 믿어진다. → The actor is believed to earn more than $10 million every year.
- 감점 포인트: to 누락, earn을 earning/earned로 변형.

**13.**
- 정답: The room was filled with colorful balloons for the party.
- 핵심 문법: by 이외의 전치사 — be filled with.
- 출제 근거: The room was filled with colorful balloons for the party.
- 감점 포인트: with 대신 by/of, filled → fill 형태 오류.

**14.**
- 정답: I am worried about the result of the math exam.
- 핵심 문법: by 이외의 전치사 — be worried about.
- 출제 근거: I am worried about the result of the math exam.
- 감점 포인트: about 대신 for/with, worried → worry.

**15.**
- 정답: The garden was covered with snow.
- 핵심 문법: by 이외의 전치사 — be covered with.
- 출제 근거: (부교재 예문) The garden was covered with snow.
- 감점 포인트: with 대신 by, covered → cover.

**16.**
- 정답: The Harry Potter series is composed of seven books.
- 핵심 문법: by 이외의 전치사 — be composed of.
- 출제 근거: (부교재 예문) The Harry Potter series is composed of seven books.
- 감점 포인트: of 대신 with/from, 단수 동사 is 대신 are(series는 단수 취급).

**17.**
- 정답: The website is being updated, so you can't use it now.
- 핵심 문법: 현재진행형 수동태 「be동사 + being + p.p.」.
- 출제 근거: (부교재 예문) 그 웹 사이트는 업데이트되고 있다. → The website is being updated, so you can't use it now.
- 감점 포인트: being 누락(is updated), updated → update/updating.

**18.**
- 정답: The washing machine is being repaired.
- 핵심 문법: 현재진행형 수동태.
- 출제 근거: (부교재 예문) The washing machine is being repaired.
- 감점 포인트: being 누락, repaired → repair.

**19.**
- 정답: I haven't been invited to the party yet.
- 핵심 문법: 현재완료형 수동태 「have/has + been + p.p.」의 부정문. invite는 to를 동반.
- 출제 근거: (부교재 예문) I haven't been invited to the party yet.

**20.**

### 객관식 (21~30번)

**21. 정답 ④**
- ① disappeared → disappeared 삭제 (disappear는 자동사이므로 수동태 불가)
- ② was seen enter → was seen entering[to enter] (지각동사 수동태 보어)
- ③ are → is (every + 단수명사 → 단수 동사 수일치)
- ⑤ is being repair → is being repaired (진행형 수동태의 과거분사)

**22. 정답 ②**
- ① bought to → bought for (buy의 4형식 수동태 전치사는 for)
- ③ made cleaning → made to clean (사역동사 수동태 보어는 to부정사)
- ④ were had → had 삭제 (have가 '소유'의 뜻일 때 수동태 불가)
- ⑤ was → were (주어 The children은 복수, 수일치)

**23. 정답 ⑤**
- ① is covered by → is covered with (be covered with)
- ② is updating → is being updated (능동형 → 진행 수동태)
- ③ by we → by us (전치사의 목적어는 목적격 대명사)
- ④ was made learn → was made to learn (사역동사 수동태 보어의 to 누락)

**24. 정답 ①**
- ② have → has (주어 The problem은 단수, 수일치)
- ③ was seen read → was seen reading[to read] (지각동사 수동태 보어)
- ④ is filled → was filled (yesterday와 시제 불일치)
- ⑤ is crowded by → is crowded with (be crowded with)

**25. 정답 ③**
- ① is always worry → is always worried (be worried about)
- ② Julian elected → Julian was elected (be동사 누락, 태 오류)
- ④ by they → by them (전치사의 목적어는 목적격)
- ⑤ is made from → is made of (성질이 변하지 않는 재료는 of)

**26. 정답 ④**
- ① is interesting → is interested (be interested in, 감정 유발이 아닌 감정 수용)
- ② haven't invited → haven't been invited (완료 수동태의 been 누락)
- ③ was → were (주어 The children은 복수, 수일치)
- ⑤ made me to learn → made me learn (사역동사 능동태의 보어는 원형부정사)

**27. 정답 ②**
- ① is being update → is being updated (진행 수동태의 과거분사)
- ③ to we → to us (전치사의 목적어는 목적격)
- ④ is said be → is said to be (to부정사의 to 누락)
- ⑤ must celebrated → must be celebrated (조동사 수동태의 be 누락)

**28. 정답 ⑤**
- ① were made stay → were made to stay (사역동사 수동태 보어는 to부정사)
- ② will placed → will be placed (조동사 수동태의 be 누락)
- ③ were → was (주어 The room은 단수, 수일치)
- ④ covered by → covered with (be covered with)

**29. 정답 ①**
- ② were had → had 삭제 (have가 '소유'일 때 수동태 불가)
- ③ given for me → given to me (give의 4형식 수동태 전치사는 to)
- ④ to reading → reading[to read] (지각동사 수동태 보어 형태)
- ⑤ every floors → every floor (every + 단수명사)

**30. 정답 ③**
- ① I was bought → The pretty mug was bought for me (buy는 간접목적어를 주어로 한 수동태 불가)
- ② worth watched → worth watching (worth + v-ing)
- ④ was seen entered → was seen entering[to enter] (지각동사 수동태 보어)
- ⑤ The actor believes to earn → The actor is believed to earn (that절 수동태 전환, 태 오류)
